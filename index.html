Terms & Protocols

Revised: March 2026

1. Scope

Royal Home Concierge Systems provides estate curation and asset management. We are system architects; we are not a cleaning service or licensed appraiser.

2. Residency & Service Caps

  • Stewardship Tiers: Capped at 12 residency days per month per estate.
  • Signature Commissions: Project-based services are strictly capped at 5 service days per month.

3. The Protocol (Privacy)

All curators are bound by strict legal NDAs. No imagery or estate data leaves the residence.

4. Liability

Claims must be reported in writing within 24 hours of session conclusion. Liability limited to the cost of the residency session in question.
