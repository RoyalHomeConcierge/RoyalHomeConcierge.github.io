FAQs

What is a Home Systems Curator? +
We are architects of order. Unlike traditional staff, we build and maintain the structural systems that govern your most personal assets.
How does the residency cap work? +
To ensure high-touch exclusivity, Stewardship Clients are limited to 12 service days per month. Single-service projects are capped at 5 days.
Do you handle physical transport? +
We perform the "Curated Edit" to identify items for removal. Physical transport and donation logistics are managed by the estate’s secondary staff.

The Protocol

NDA & Privacy: All curators are bound by strict legal privacy contracts. Estate data never leaves the residence.

The 8-Hour Window: Each service day is a dedicated professional residency of 8 hours.

Terms

Scheduling: 72-hour notice required for all cancellations. Late changes are billed at the full daily rate.

Materials: Archival materials (hangers, bins, labels) are curated and billed separately as estate assets.
